Abuja Bus Terminal Project

Modern Bus & Taxi Terminal in CBD!

Federal Capital Territory Adminitration (FCTA) is in the process of building modern bus and taxi terminal in its communities. Abuja's Central Business District is one the communities to benefit from the FCT Administration's broader strategy to revamp the capital's infrastructure. The terminal, a state-of-the-art facility, will include amenities such as restaurants and relaxation areas designed to offer commuters both convenience and comfort.
